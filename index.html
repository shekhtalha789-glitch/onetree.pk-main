How It Works

From a simple gift to a verified climate contribution — four steps that make a real difference across Pakistan.

Step 01
Receive Your Plantable Gift
Customers receive an eco-friendly pack containing a sapling and a unique QR code linked to their digital tree profile.
Step 02
Register Your Tree
Scan the QR code and register your tree with a photo, GPS location, and a brief story to create your verified digital asset.
Step 03
Track Its Growth
Upload photos every few months. Our AI verifies health and growth milestones, keeping your tree's journey alive on the platform.
Step 04
Become a Green Champion
After verified survival, earn rewards, recognition badges, and join our growing community of Pakistani climate champions.
